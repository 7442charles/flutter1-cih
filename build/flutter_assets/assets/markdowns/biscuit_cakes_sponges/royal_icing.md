| Ingredients           | Quantity               |
|-----------------------|------------------------|
| Icing Sugar           | 400g                   |
| Pasteurised egg whites| 3                      |
| Lemon, juice          | 1                      |
| Glycerine             | 2tsp                   |

### **Method of work**
1. Sift the icing sugar. Mix it well with the egg whites in a basin, using a wooden spoon.
2. Add a few drops of lemon juice and glycerine and beat until stiff.
