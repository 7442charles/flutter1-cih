### **Ingredients**
- 100g Butter
- 100g Icing sugar
- 100g Flour
- 2 Egg whites

### **Method of work**
1. Mix all ingredients; allow to rest for 1 hour.
2. Spread to the required shape and size.
3. Bake at approx. 200–210 °C.
4. While hot, mould the biscuits to the required shape and leave to cool.
