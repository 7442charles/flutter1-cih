|Ingredients|Quantity|
|-----------|--------|
|Icing sugar|150g|
|Butter|200g|


### **Method of work**
1. Sieve the icing sugar.
2. Cream the butter and icing sugar until light and creamy.
3. Flavour and colour as required.
