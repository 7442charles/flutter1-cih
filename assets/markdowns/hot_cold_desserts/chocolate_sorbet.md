|Ingredients|Quantity|
|----------|---------|
|skimmed milk|100ml|
|Sugar|150g|
|Water|400ml|
|Ice cream stabiliser|40g|
|Cocoa Powder|30g|
|Dark Couverture|60g|

**Method of work**
1. Combine the water, milk, sugar, stabiliser and cocoa powder. Bring to the boil slowly. Simmer for 5 minutes.
2. Add the couverture and allow to cool.
3. Pass and churn.
