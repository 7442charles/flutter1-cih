|ingredients|Quantity|
|-----------|--------|
|Egg yolks, Patteurised|6|
|Caster or unrefined sugar|100g|
|Dry White wine|250ml|


**Method of work**
1. Whisk the egg yolks and sugar in a 1-litre pan or basin until white.
2. Dilute with the wine.
3. Place the pan or basin in a bain-marie of boiling water.
4. Whisk the mixture continuously until it increases its bulk by four times and is firm and frothy.
